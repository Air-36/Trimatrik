About Us

At Trimatrik, we empower engineers, educators, and students to visualize innovation in 3D. Our hologram table revolutionizes learning and design by bringing TCAD simulations of MOSFETs, SolidWorks models, and interactive 3D educational content to life.

By bridging the gap between virtual simulations and real-world understanding, we foster collaborative problem-solving and accelerate mastery in engineering, JEE, NEET, and beyond. With cutting-edge holography, we’re shaping the future of immersive education—one projection at a time.
